<ol class="list-decimal pl-6 mt-4">
<li>the country or countries where the work will be carried out and the length of service abroad;</li>
<li>the currency in which the wage will be paid;</li>

<li>whether compensation is paid for travel home and, if so, conditions for travel home.</li>
</ol> <p>For postings under Section 23 of the Posting of Workers Act (1999:678), the information must also contain the following details:</p>

<li>additional compensation associated with the posting and regulations on compensation for expenses for travel, board and lodging, where applicable; and</li>
<li>a link to the host country’s national website in accordance with Article 5(2) of Directive 2014/67/EU of the European Parliament and of the Council of 15 May 2014 on the enforcement of Directive 96/71/EC concerning the posting of workers in the framework of the provision of services and amending Regulation (EU) No 1024/2012 on administrative cooperation through the International Market Information System (‘the IMI Regulation’), in the original wording.</li>
